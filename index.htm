Lofty Lagoon

Welcome

In a peaceful world among a large collection of floating islands, islanders of each have formed their own small communities.

Lofty Lagoon is a game about exploring these islands, building relationships with the locals and making a name for yourself.

Seek out adventure or relax by a calm lake. Trade with the other islanders or avoid having to interact with them at all cost. The world is your oyster.

This is a personal project of mine so progress on it can be rather slow. Over time more information will be made available on this website.

You can find some of the game's music tracks and development screenshots down below.

- Christiaan Bakker.
